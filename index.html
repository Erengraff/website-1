Travel to Weymouth!

See all kinds of animals, like...

  • Dogs!
  • More dogs!
  • Cats!
  • Foxes!

Tourists from all around the world come to Weymouth to gawk at the local wildlife. There are plenty of dogs to see, as well as cats, foxes, and (not pictured) raccoons. Many kinds of birds live here, too. Ones with blue feathers, red feathers, big beaks, small beaks. Sometimes the other animals eat them, which is sad for the birds. But even predators have tummies to fill and babies to take care of. It's just a matter of perspective.

And different places, such as...

  1. Lakes!
  2. Clouds!
  3. Gardens!
  4. Grass!

Though we don't have much in the way of tourist locations, Weymouth does have beauty. For example, trees are abundant here. They come in all shapes and sizes. There are lakes, ponds, and rivers that sparkle in the sunlight. Pretty flowers grow in the more rural areas. And if you look, you can see streaks of pink and purple clouds at dusk.

A Summary

Weymouth is a great place to visit if you like the suburban scenery. Sure, there aren't pyramids or monuments, but it's a nice change of pace. See all sorts of common animals and places that are beautiful in their simplicity. It's not far from the city, and it's small enough that you can get around quickly. Try going there sometime. Maybe you'll learn something new.

-Eren
